Eagle Mark Holding (EHL) Ltd

Eagle Mark Holding (EHL) Ltd is a private limited company established on August 8, 2008, with the objective of delivering reliable, high-quality solutions that make a positive impact on both our clients and the communities we serve.

EHL is dedicated to empowering Rwandan youth with the skills and experience to thrive by creating sustainable job opportunities across various industries.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To be a vanguard in the Rwandan private sector success, while also improving the country's overall socio-economic operations.

Our Vision

To build a strong and reputable presence in industries we operate in, driven by a commitment to excellence, customer satisfaction, and continuous innovation. We strive to create long-term value for our stakeholders while positively impacting the market we serve.
